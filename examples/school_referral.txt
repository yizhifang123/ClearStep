CONFIDENTIAL — STUDENT SUPPORT SERVICES NOTICE

TO: Parent/Guardian of student (Grade 10)
FROM: School Counseling Office
RE: Recommendation for outside support services

Following a check-in initiated by classroom staff regarding observed changes
in mood, engagement, and attendance, the counseling office recommends that the
family pursue an outside mental health evaluation. Per district protocol, the
school is not able to provide ongoing clinical treatment and is providing this
referral so the family may seek services in the community.

Enclosed: a list of community providers and the district's Student Assistance
Program (SAP) contact. Please note that a Release of Information will be
required before the school can coordinate with any outside provider.

If you believe your child is in immediate danger, do not wait for an
appointment — seek emergency help.

We are available to discuss support and accommodations (including a possible
504 plan) at your request.
